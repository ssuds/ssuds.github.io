About Me



I've had the opportunity to apply my skills in very diverse roles ranging from research and development for electrical systems and large construction equipment to the production and testing of complex aerospace structures and propulsion systems.

This exposure allows me to approach engineering problems with a full understanding of the entire product lifecycle: initial concept generation, manufacturing of a finished product, optimizations after product release and everything in between. As all the companies I've worked for have had on-site manufacturing capacity, I understand exactly how CAD models translate into an actual product.

Currently, I'm working at Blue Origin developing the engines to land the first woman and next man on the moon.

I'm always open to new opportunities allowing me to apply my skills to make a real impact on a product, process, or society as a whole.
