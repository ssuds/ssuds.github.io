About Me



I'm currently pursuing a bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, graduating in 2017.

On campus, I'm heavily involved with our school's Formula SAE team, Illini Motorsports. Through my time on the team, I've been able to grow from a new recruit into the engine team leader. This year, I'm responsible for coordinating a full redesign of our vehicle's engine package.

Outside of school, I've had the opportunity to apply my skills in very diverse roles ranging from research and development for electrical systems and large construction equipment to the production of complex aerospace structures. This exposure allows me to approach engineering problems with a full understanding of the entire product lifecycle: initial concept generation, manufacturing of a finished product, optimizations after product release and everything in between. As all the companies I've worked for have had on-site manufacturing capacity, I understand exactly how CAD models translate into an actual product.

I'm always open to new opportunities allowing me to apply my skills to make a real impact on a product, process, or society as a whole.
